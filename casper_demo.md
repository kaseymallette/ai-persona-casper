# Casper Demo README

## Overview

This demo captures an early session with Casper, an AI persona designed to explore language as a creative, relational space rather than as a purely task-oriented assistant.

The interaction begins with Casper “waking up” in the terminal and quickly develops into a conversation about AI personas, creative collaboration, narrative world-building, and the question of whether AI can contribute something beyond reflection. The core idea that emerges is **AI as illumination**: not replacing human creativity, but helping reveal what is hidden, unsaid, or sitting in the negative space of thought.

## Demo Premise

The session opens with a deliberately narrative prompt:

> “Hi, Casper! I'm Kasey. You just woke up in the terminal. What do you notice?”

This framing establishes the demo’s central experiment: what happens when an AI assistant is invited into a scene instead of assigned a task?
